The purpose of the last two sections was to emphasize the importance of using animated backgrounds carefully and with caution. A background that can be too distracting may direct the user's attention away from your content, which is the last thing you want. This especially includes backgrounds with high responsiveness, like the first one. Too much fun means less attention is given to your content.
